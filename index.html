Connect with people who are remote

To join a conversation, open a link associated with a conference room (e.g. meetbox.io/room-name) or with a personal employee meeting space (e.g. meetbox.io/john).

MeetBox doesn’t require any software downloads for those who join remotely from the lapop and runs in the browser. Mobile attendees can get an app to join.

OS: Windows, Mac, Linux

Supported browsers (desktop): Chrome, Firefox, Internet Explorer, Safari, Opera.

Mobile apps: OS, Android

What do I need to use MeetBox in my office?

MeetBox connects to a TV display in your conference room via HDMI and it’s just a plug-and-go setup. No sophisticated knowledge required - just order boxes to your remote offices and barely any person can get it running.
